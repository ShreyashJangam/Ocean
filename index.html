Volunteer Spotlight: Maria's Coral Restoration Journey

Marine biologist Maria Santos has led our coral restoration project in the Philippines for three years, successfully rehabilitating over 500 hectares of damaged reef. Her innovative approach combines traditional ecological knowledge with cutting-edge science, involving local fishing communities as active partners in conservation. The project has not only restored marine biodiversity but also increased fish populations, directly benefiting the livelihoods of over 1,200 local fishers.
